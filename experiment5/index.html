Description

What are you trying to acomplish with this experiment?

I'm trying to make more abstract art with squares, polygons, and some ascii text for the image.

Technical

Describe in detail what you did technically and how it works?

This draws shapes over the original image and puts a background on it. The original colors of the image are applied and the shapes are created. Slider is there to mutate the photos and then you can save the best image with the best image button.
